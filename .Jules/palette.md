## 2025-05-14 - [Confirmation Dialogs and Tooltips]
**Learning:** Confirmation dialogs for destructive actions like task deletion significantly reduce accidental data loss and improve user confidence. Tooltips on icon-only buttons are essential for accessibility and clarity, especially when the icon's meaning isn't universally obvious.
**Action:** Always implement a confirmation step for any action that deletes or permanently modifies user data. Ensure all icon-only buttons have a descriptive tooltip or ARIA label.
